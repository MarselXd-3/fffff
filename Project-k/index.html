Novel

Solo Leveling, also alternatively translated as Only I Level Up (Korean: 나 혼자만 레벨업; RR: Na Honjaman Rebeleop), is a South Korean web novel written by Chugong. It was serialized in Kakao's digital comic and fiction platform KakaoPage beginning on July 25, 2016, and was later published in by D&C Media under their Papyrus label since November 4, 2016. The novel has been licensed in English by Yen Press

Manhwa

Solo Leveling (나 혼자만 레벨업, Na Honjaman Lebel-eob) “Solo Leveling Manhwa” or “Solo Leveling Manga” is a Korean webtoon series written by Chu-Gong and illustrated by Gi Si-Ryeong and Jang Su-Rak.

Anime

Solo Leveling is an anime television series based on the South Korean web novel of the same name written by Chugong. It was produced by A-1 Pictures and directed by Shunsuke Nakashige, with Noboru Kimura writing the scripts, Tomoko Sudo designing the characters, and Hiroyuki Sawano composing the music.
